Enhancing LMS Design through SUS Analysis and Biometric Usability Testing

2025 ECTI DAMT & NCON Conference · IEEE

Conference paper

Conducted usability testing on an LMS website using SUS surveys and biometric analysis to find usability issues and recommend design improvements.

  • Conducted SUS analysis and biometric usability testing on the MFU LMS website.
  • Collected and analyzed survey responses from 417 participants.

Generative AI in UX Education: Assessing Critical Thinking, Problem Solving, and Design Iteration Speed

2025 9th International Conference on Information Technology (InCIT) · IEEE

Conference paper

Investigated the impact of Generative AI tools on UX/UI education by evaluating students' critical thinking, problem-solving ability, and design iteration speed during the design process.

  • Researched the role of Generative AI in UX/UI education and design workflows.
  • Analyzed how AI-assisted design influences critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Evaluated improvements in design iteration speed through human-AI collaboration.
  • Conducted statistical analysis and interpretation of educational outcomes.
  • Published findings in an IEEE international conference proceeding.
